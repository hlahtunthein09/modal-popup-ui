Chaeyoung

Chaeyoung is Twice’s main rapper and a talented singer-songwriter. Known for her unique charm and artistic style, she brings a bold, edgy energy to the group. Her expressive performances and creative personality make her stand out on stage and off.

Nayeon

Nayeon is the oldest member of Twice and the group’s main vocalist. She is cheerful, energetic, and has a bright personality that lights up the stage. Known for her strong vocals and captivating presence, Nayeon is often the face of Twice.

Sana

Sana is Twice’s sweetheart, famous for her adorable charm and playful personality. Her signature “aegyo” and bright smile have won the hearts of fans worldwide. Sana shines on stage with her charisma and lovable energy.
